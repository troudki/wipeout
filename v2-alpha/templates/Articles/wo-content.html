The Content attribute

wo-content="value"

data-wo-content="value"

The content attribute defines the html content of an element.

The content attribute is similar to the attibute but is less powerful. As a rule of thumb, you should use content to display simple things such as strings, numbers and simple objects and render to display view models or items which are not surrounded by a html tag {{$this.someValue}}.

<wo.content model--s="Hello Wipeout!"> <set-template> <span wo-content="$model"></span> </set-template> </wo.content> <wo.view template-id="'wo-content-attr'"></wo.view>
