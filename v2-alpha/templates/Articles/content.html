Setting the template within a template

Within wipeout it is often useful to set the template within another template, rather than by id. To do this you can use the view model.

Nested content controls: <wo.content> <set-template> Content control 1 <div style="padding-left: 20px;"> <wo.content> <set-template> Content control 2 <div style="padding-left: 20px;"> <wo.content> <set-template> Content control 3 </set-template> </wo.content> </div> </set-template> </wo.content> </div> </set-template> </wo.content> Renders: <wo.view template-id="'content-article-1'"></wo.view>
