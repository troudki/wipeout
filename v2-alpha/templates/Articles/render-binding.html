Render binding

The render binding is used internally in wipeout to render viewModels, and can also be used in application development

window.renderVm = wo.content.extend(function renderVm() { this._super(); this.innerItem = new wo.content(); this.innerItem.template("Hello wipeout!"); }); <render-vm> <set-template> <div data-bind="render: innerItem"></div> </set-template> </render-vm> <wo.view template-id="'render-binding-script-1'" />
