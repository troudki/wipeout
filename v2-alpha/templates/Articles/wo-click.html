The Click attribute

wo-click="eventHandler"

data-wo-click="eventHandler"

The click attribute defines an event handler to be fired when the element is clicked.

<wo.content model--s="Hello Wipeout!"> <set-template> <button wo-click="logic: alert($model)">Click me</button> </set-template> </wo.content> <wo.view template-id="'wo-click-attr'"></wo.view>
