Find and call

$findAndCall(args) is shorthand for $call($context.find(args))

<myViewModel model="'Hello wipeout!'"> <set-template> <wo.content> <set-template> <button data-bind="$findAndCall({$i: myViewModel}).dot('model').dot('submit').args(true)"> Submit</button> </set-template> </wo.content> </set-template> </myViewModel>
